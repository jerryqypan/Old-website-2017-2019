Duke Textbook Exchange

HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, PHP

For our final project in CS190s, my group built a website that facilitates the exchange of textbooks between students at Duke. The website allows students to create an account and post listings of textbooks they finished using and allows other students to search the listings and contact the seller of the textbook they want.
